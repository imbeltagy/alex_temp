Spearheading the convergence of finance and technology

There has been a time in history when banking, finance and insurance has undergone a sea change. Technology has transformed all these services into digital offerings with an economic edge to them. In the proces, a new breed of technology that converges both finance and technology has come into existence - FinTech. FinTech enables everybody from households that want to build corpus for their retirement to CFOs that want to revamp the financial operations of their businesses a novel way of functioning.

Intelivita has been dabbling with FinTech for quite sometime now. We were fascinated by its ability to bring financial inclusion to all equally. As a digital corporation, Intelivita believes that banks, insurers, financial institutions and customers can partake in the benefits of financial transformation.
